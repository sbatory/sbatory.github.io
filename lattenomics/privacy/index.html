Lattenomics — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Lattenomics converts a price into cups of your favorite coffee drink. It works entirely on your device: there are no user accounts, no sign-in, and we operate no servers that receive your data.

Data you enter stays on your device

The few things Lattenomics remembers — your chosen drink, state, custom drink name and price, manual price override, and appearance preference — are stored only in the app's local storage on your iPhone. The prices you type in to convert are not stored at all. We cannot see any of it.

No network requests

The app makes no internet requests of any kind. Its drink and price catalog is built into the app itself, so nothing you do in Lattenomics ever leaves your device.

What we don't do

  • No analytics, telemetry, or crash-reporting SDKs
  • No advertising and no ad identifiers
  • No tracking across apps or websites
  • No selling or sharing of data — we never possess it in the first place

TestFlight betas

If you use a beta version through Apple's TestFlight, Apple may share standard beta feedback and crash logs with us as described in Apple's privacy policy. These contain diagnostic information, not your in-app settings.

Deleting your data

Delete the app — the handful of settings it stored goes with it.
